# Retrospective

Conduct a retrospective on the completed work.

## Reflection Questions

### What Went Well
- What parts of the process worked smoothly?
- What decisions proved effective?
- What tools or practices helped?

### What Could Improve
- Where did things get stuck or delayed?
- What was confusing or unclear?
- What would make this easier next time?

### Lessons Learned
- What did we learn about the codebase?
- What did we learn about the process?
- What should we remember for future work?

### Metrics
- How long did the work take?
- How many revisions were needed?
- What was the scope vs. initial plan?

## Action Items
Identify any follow-up items:
- Process improvements to implement
- Documentation to update
- Patterns to establish
- Knowledge to share with the team

Document the retrospective findings for team knowledge.
